L001: All documents filed must be paginated consecutively.
L002: An index must be included at the front of the bundle.
L003: Each exhibit should be separated with a tab or electronic bookmark.
L004: Authorities bundles should include only the pages relied upon.
L005: Where a party relies on an unreported decision, the full text should be provided.
L006: The hearing bundle must be submitted no later than 3 working days before the hearing date.
L007: Sensitive personal data must be redacted unless the court orders otherwise.
L008: If redaction is applied, a version log must indicate what was redacted and why.
L009: All references in submissions should cite paragraph numbers where available.
L010: If paragraph numbers are unavailable, cite page and line numbers.
