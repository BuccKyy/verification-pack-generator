L001: Confidential Information includes any client documents not publicly available.
L002: Do not upload Confidential Information to third-party services without written approval.
L003: Use of external AI tools is prohibited for Confidential Information unless an on-prem deployment is used.
L004: Access to case materials must follow least-privilege principles.
L005: Audit logs must record who accessed materials and when.
L006: Retention of working files should be minimised and aligned with matter requirements.
L007: If a dataset is created for evaluation, it must be anonymised where feasible.
L008: Anonymisation must remove names, NRIC numbers, addresses, and other direct identifiers.
L009: If anonymisation is not feasible, the dataset must remain within a restricted environment.
L010: Any incident of accidental disclosure must be reported within 24 hours.
